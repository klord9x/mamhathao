Atlantic Cedar Exfoliant Paste

Your skin reacts different in the winter from summer, our products treat your skin with the proper vitamins for every season so it's healthy year-round.

  • Skin Touch Cleansed, Refreshing, Gently Moist
  • Texture Light yet nourishing gel-cream
  • Scent Warm, woody, resinous

How to Use

After cleansing and toning, apply generously to the face and neck.
For best results, leave on the skin overnight and rinse in the morning during routine cleansing.
